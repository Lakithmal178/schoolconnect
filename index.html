About Our School

Dharmaraja College, established in 1887 in Kandy, Sri Lanka, is renowned for combining traditional Buddhist values with modern education to nurture well-rounded individuals. The school emphasizes discipline, leadership, and academic success.

Our curriculum integrates science, arts, and technology, supported by experienced teachers and state-of-the-art facilities. We are committed to encouraging students to excel in all spheres of life.

Our Mission

To empower students with knowledge, ethical values, and skills to become responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in education that inspires innovation, compassion, and lifelong learning.
